WELCOME TO Multi Disease Classification

This application leverages machine learning models to predict various diseases:

  • Diabetes
  • Heart Disease
  • Parkinson's Disease

Disease Information

Diabetes

A chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose), leading to high sugar levels.

Heart Disease

A progressive neurological disorder that affects movement, causing tremors, stiffness, and difficulty with balance.

Parkinson's Disease

A broad term for conditions affecting the heart, including blocked arteries, heart attacks, and heart failure.

About Project

Multi Disease Classification is an AI-driven approach that analyzes medical data to predict and classify multiple diseases, such as diabetes, Parkinson's, and heart disease. It helps in early detection and diagnosis, improving patient care and treatment planning.

  • Support Vector Machine (SVM):

    Finds the best decision boundary to classify diseases with high accuracy.

  • Decision Tree:

    Splits data into branches based on conditions to make predictions in a simple, interpretable way.

  • Random Forest:

    Uses multiple decision trees to improve accuracy and reduce overfitting for better disease classification.
